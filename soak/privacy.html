    • Orders & Shipping

    All orders will be shipped 1 week after the drop date. Worldwide shipping is avaliable. Depending on your location, shipping may take anywhere between 3-30 days. For more information on shipping times please contact orders@soak.com. Shipping and handling rates are based on weight and destination. Tracking confirmation will be sent via email once your order has shipped. If you do not recieve a confirmation email please contact orders@soak.com and we will assist you.

    Shipping addresses cannot be changed once your order has been placed. We are not responsible for any lost items during shipping or delivery. Contact your carrier to locate your package if needed.


    Privacy Policy

    We only collect your email address you use to place orders or sign up to our mailing list with. We will never collect or save any other information about you. We do not use any tracking technologies or cookies and will never share your data for tracking purposes. If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy or the use of your data then please contact us at privacy@soak.com.
